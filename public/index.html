About PlacEdge

PlacEdge is an AI-powered placement preparation assistant designed to help students improve their chances of success in campus placements. It provides smart support for resume analysis, interview practice, HR answer improvement, skill gap analysis, and placement readiness checking.

The goal of PlacEdge is to guide students in identifying their strengths, improving weak areas, and preparing confidently for placement drives. With a simple and user-friendly interface, it acts as a virtual mentor for career preparation.
